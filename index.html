How It Works

Check your event weather and risk in three simple steps

1

Enter Location & Date

Provide your event location using the advanced Nominatim search and select your specific event date.

2

Analyze Weather Data

We query the Open-Meteo API for a 7-day forecast, including precipitation, wind, and temperature data.

3

Evaluate Risk Score

A custom algorithm determines the event's risk level and provides a clear color-coded summary.
